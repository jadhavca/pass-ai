How It Works

Up and running in under ten minutes

Parents set everything up. Children just learn.

1
Create a parent account

Sign up with Google or email. Add your child's name, target school, and exam date. No card needed during the trial.

2
Your child takes a short assessment

A friendly 15-minute diagnostic covers all core 11+ topics. A personalised preparation plan is built immediately.

3
Daily practice, guided by AI

30–45 minutes per day. The AI adapts as your child improves, always focusing where it matters most.

4
You track everything

Weekly progress reports land in your inbox. You see exactly how ready your child is and what to prioritise.
